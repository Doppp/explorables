# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation in `explorables` a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, colour,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Contributors should use welcoming language, respect differing viewpoints,
accept constructive feedback, focus on what is best for learners and the
community, and show empathy. Harassment, sexualised attention, trolling,
personal attacks, publishing private information, and other conduct reasonably
considered inappropriate are unacceptable.

Project maintainers may clarify standards, remove or reject contributions, and
temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behaviour is harmful.
Report conduct concerns privately through the repository owner's GitHub
profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially, with action
proportionate to impact and context.

This policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
